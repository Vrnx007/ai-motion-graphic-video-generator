# Concierge MVP — Motion AI (SaaS demo wedge)

## Offer (one sentence)

**We deliver a premium AI-assisted SaaS explainer or feature tour in 24–72 hours**, using your live marketing site, curated screenshots, and your brand kit — with one included revision.

## Ideal customer

- Seed–Series B SaaS founder or PMM who needs a **homepage hero video**, **launch clip**, or **2–3 minute feature tour**.
- Has a real marketing site + product screenshots (not figma-only).

## Intake checklist (send to client)

1. Primary URL + any alternate locales.
2. 5–10 screenshots (PNG) of the product UI they want on screen.
3. Logo SVG/PNG and brand hex codes (if not obvious from the site).
4. Aspect + length target (e.g. 30s 16:9 for site hero, 45s 9:16 for paid social).
5. Single-line positioning (“We help X do Y without Z”).
6. Must-show features (max 5 bullets).
7. Legal: permission to scrape their public site and to use their marks in a paid deliverable.

## Delivery package

- Studio project saved in their workspace (or handover as agreed).
- Share link for stakeholder review (opaque `shareToken` URL).
- WebM master from in-browser capture; optional MP4 when `REMOTION_RENDER_WEBHOOK_URL` worker is configured.
- One revision round (timing, copy on specific scenes, or swap template class for a scene).

## Pricing bands (suggested)

- **$299–$599** — Short hero (≤20s), single pass, light motion.
- **$799–$1,499** — Full explainer (30–90s), multi-scene, brand curation, one revision.
- **$2k+** — Rush, extra revision, or custom template work.

Adjust for your market; keep minimum high enough that manual QA is sustainable.

## SLA

- First cut: 24–72h from complete intake.
- Revision: 24h after feedback.

## What you learn (log every deal)

- Which templates clients loved vs rejected.
- Typical scene count and duration.
- Whether they needed MP4 or accepted WebM.
- Price sensitivity and competitor references.

Feed those notes back into prompt and template iterations (`docs/internal/BENCHMARK_RUBRIC.md`).
